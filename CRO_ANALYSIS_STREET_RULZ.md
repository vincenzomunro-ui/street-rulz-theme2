---

## 📊 **CURRENT HOMEPAGE AUDIT**

### ✅ WHAT'S WORKING
1. **Clear value prop:** "Queenslands Best Caged Football Pitches"
2. **Multiple service options:** Pitch hire, leagues, events, parties
3. **Pricing transparency:** $110-140/hr clearly shown
4. **Social proof:** Team ladder, Google reviews
5. **Good imagery:** Action shots of gameplay
6. **Easy navigation:** Clear CTAs to book/competitions

### ❌ CONVERSION BLOCKERS

1. **HERO SECTION ISSUES:**
   - Hero text is generic ("Street Rulz Football")
   - No compelling reason to book NOW
   - No urgency or scarcity messaging
   - CTA says "Book a Pitch" (weak call-to-action verb)
   - Missing "Why choose us" messaging

2. **VALUE PROP CONFUSION:**
   - Multiple service cards (Pitch hire, Leagues, Fiesta, Birthdays)
   - Unclear what primary offering is
   - "Fiesta" is confusing (event vs league?)
   - Pricing scattered across sections

3. **LACK OF TRUST SIGNALS:**
   - No testimonials/reviews early in page
   - No social proof (booking count, player count)
   - No certification/credentials
   - No guarantees

4. **WEAK MOBILE EXPERIENCE:**
   - Long sections on mobile = scroll fatigue
   - Hero badge text might be cut off
   - Stats bar (Footwear, 5v5, 7v7) is confusing on mobile
   - Pricing section is below fold on mobile

5. **BOOKING FRICTION:**
   - Booking process not shown (seems complex)
   - No clear "what happens next"
   - "Book in 60 seconds" claim not backed up
   - No reassurance before booking

6. **MISSING URGENCY:**
   - No availability indicators
   - No "limited slots" messaging
   - No time-sensitive offers
   - Events section has one "REGO STILL OPEN" but messaging weak

7. **STATS BAR CONFUSION:**
   - "Footwear" (with label "OUTDOOR BOOTS") = confusing
   - "5v5" / "7v7" = format unclear
   - Should be: "Players" / "Bookings" / "Leagues" / "Events"

8. **CLUTTERED LAYOUT:**
   - Too many sections (hero, stats, services, image-break, what's-on, ladder, pricing, how-it-works, find-us, hours, CTA, reviews)
   - Information overload on first visit
   - No clear "scannable" hierarchy

9. **WEAK CALL-TO-ACTION COPY:**
   - "Book a Pitch — From $110/hr" (transactional, not emotional)
   - "Join a League" (fine but secondary)
   - "Book Weekday" / "Book Weekend" (repetitive, weak)

10. **NO COMPETITIVE DIFFERENTIATION:**
    - Why Street Rulz vs other Gold Coast pitches?
    - "Elite Football Meets Street Culture" (vague)
    - No mention of: location, all-weather, professional setup, coaching, tournaments
